What is Vanilla Extract?

  Vanilla extract is obtained by macerating the cured beans in a solution of water and alcohol. It might contain sugar or glycerine as sweeteners or thickeners [5].

Conventional vanilla extracts have a minimum ethanol content of 35%, and contain the soluble extractives from 1 part by weight of vanilla beans in 10 parts by volume of hydroalcoholic solution. [5].
