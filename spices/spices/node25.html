How do I differentiate between real and unreal vanilla extract?

``The two best indicators of pure vanilla extract are alcohol content and price. The alcohol content must be at least 35%; synthetics usually have no alcohol or at most, about 2%. Any purchases that cost less than US$25.00 a quart are most likely synthetic.''[7]
